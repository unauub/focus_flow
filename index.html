How it works

Your productivity boost in 3 simple steps

01

Plan your tasks

Easily add your to-dos to a list and set priorities to create a clear plan for your day.
so you always know what to work on first.

02

Work with focus

Start the Pomodoro timer and dedicate the next 25 minutes to a single task, blocking out distractions.
This helps you build momentum and get meaningful work done without getting sidetracked.

03

Track your progress

Take a short break after each session, then repeat.
As you complete more tasks, you'll see your accomplishments build up, motivating you to stay on track.
